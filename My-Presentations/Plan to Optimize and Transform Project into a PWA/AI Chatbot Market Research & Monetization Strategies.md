## Enterprise AI Pricing Models

### **1. Subscription-Based Pricing**
**Structure**: Fixed monthly/annual fee per user or organization

**Tiers**:
- **Free/Freemium**: $0 (limited features, usage caps)
- **Professional**: $20-50/user/month
- **Business**: $50-200/user/month
- **Enterprise**: $200-500+/user/month (custom pricing)

**Advantages**:
- Predictable revenue
- Easy to understand
- Scales with team size

**Examples**: Most SaaS AI tools

---

### **2. Usage-Based Pricing**
**Structure**: Pay per API call, token, message, or interaction

**Typical Rates**:
- **Per Message**: $0.01-0.10 per message
- **Per Token**: $0.0001-0.001 per token
- **Per API Call**: $0.001-0.01 per call

**Advantages**:
- Fair for variable usage
- Scales with value
- Attracts small users

**Challenges**:
- Unpredictable costs for customers
- Enterprise buyers pushing back on this model

---

### **3. Outcome-Based Pricing**
**Structure**: Payment tied to business results

**Models**:
- Revenue share (% of sales generated)
- Cost savings share (% of costs reduced)
- Performance bonuses (hitting KPIs)

**Typical Rates**:
- 10-30% of attributed revenue
- 20-40% of cost savings

**Advantages**:
- Aligns incentives
- Reduces customer risk
- Higher potential revenue

---

### **4. Hybrid Models**
**Structure**: Combination of subscription + usage + outcomes

**Example Structure**:
- Base subscription: $1,000/month
- Usage overage: $0.05 per message above quota
- Performance bonus: 5% of revenue generated

**Advantages**:
- Balances predictability and scalability
- Captures multiple value streams

---

### **5. Per-Agent Pricing**
**Structure**: Charge per AI agent deployed

**Typical Rates**:
- $50-500 per agent per month
- Varies by agent complexity and capabilities

**Advantages**:
- Clear unit economics
- Scales with automation

---

### **6. Freemium with Premium Features**
**Structure**: Free basic tier with paid upgrades

**Conversion Rates**:
- Typical freemium conversion: 2-5%
- AI tools seeing: 3-8% conversion
- Enterprise tools: 10-15% conversion

**Premium Features**:
- Advanced AI models
- Higher usage limits
- Priority support
- Custom integrations
- White-labeling
- Analytics & reporting

---

### **7. Enterprise Custom Contracts**
**Structure**: Negotiated deals for large organizations

**Typical Deal Sizes**:
- **Mid-Market**: $50K-250K annually
- **Enterprise**: $250K-2M annually
- **Strategic Accounts**: $2M-10M+ annually

**Components**:
- Volume discounts
- SLA guarantees
- Dedicated support
- Custom features
- Professional services

## Monetization Strategy Insights

### **Key Trends (2025-2026)**

1. **Shift Toward Predictability**
   - Enterprise buyers rejecting pure usage-based pricing
   - Demand for cost predictability
   - Salesforce and others moving to hybrid models

2. **AI Feature Unbundling**
   - Separate pricing for AI features
   - Add-on model becoming standard
   - Premium AI tiers emerging

3. **Proof of Value First**
   - Customers pay when they see clear ROI
   - Free trials extended (30-90 days)
   - POC (Proof of Concept) before contracts

4. **Multi-Model Pricing**
   - Different models for different customer segments
   - SMB: Subscription
   - Mid-Market: Hybrid
   - Enterprise: Custom + Outcome-based
