# Feature Document: Deep Research Engine

## Overview

Deep Research is ShadowTalk's multi-source research engine that performs comprehensive analysis across multiple data sources, providing cited, structured research reports — similar to Perplexity Pro but integrated directly into the platform.

## How It Works

1. User submits a research query
2. The engine breaks the query into sub-queries
3. Multiple sources are searched simultaneously (web, academic, news)
4. Results are synthesized into a structured report with citations
5. The report includes key findings, analysis, and recommendations

## Key Capabilities

| Capability | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Multi-Source Search** | Web, academic papers, news, financial data |
| **Citation Tracking** | Every claim is linked to its source |
| **Structured Output** | Executive summary, key findings, detailed analysis |
| **Industry Context** | Research is filtered through the user's industry persona |
| **Export** | Download as PDF, Markdown, or presentation slides |

## Use Cases

- **Finance:** SEC filing analysis, market sentiment reports, competitor research
- **Legal:** Case law research, regulatory compliance checks, precedent analysis
- **Healthcare:** Clinical research reviews, drug interaction analysis, guidelines
- **Technology:** Tech stack evaluations, market sizing, competitive landscapes
- **Academic:** Literature reviews, systematic analyses, thesis research
